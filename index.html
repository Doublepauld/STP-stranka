About the game

this game is a 2D, top-down, pixel-art, dungeon run game. that has randomly generated leves and randomly generated open world. In the dungeon that has 100 floors, you will find a variety of enemies, which you will need to fight in order to level up or find better equipment. Every 10 floors you will find a miniBoss that after defeating him will grant you access to the open world where you can find village that will have shops whre you will be able to buy some upgrades or new equipment. You will also have access to a variety of spell and skills that will help you on your journey to the top.

About us

We are a group of students from Czech Republic that decided to make our first game. This project gave us important knowledge and insight into the game development world. We also gained skills in some parts of game development we have never done for example : random generation, design of the world and enemies and finally Teamwork in a small team.
